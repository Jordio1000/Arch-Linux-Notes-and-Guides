This is a place for me to store my notes and guides on my experiences with Arch Linux. Below you will find links to any guides I have written as well as some more free flowing notes that I feel are helpful.

DISCLAIMER: I am not an Arch Linux expert. I am just an unemployed Systems Administrator who wanted to switch to daily driving Linux and decided to make it a bit of a project by learning Arch Linux. This was my first time installing and using Arch and I just decided to document the process from what I learned through trial and error. Please do not treat this guide as gospel and do your own research on what configurations work best for you. One of the main things I learned from this experience is that there are many different ways to achieve the same goal and they all can be valid for their own reason.
